Bio

Jason is an aspiring graphic designer with a passion for creating visually stunning designs. He has honed his skills through years of practicing art and expanding his knowledge into the field of graphic design.

Since launching his YouTube channel in 2019, Jason has garnered a dedicated following of over 700 subscribers by uploading design-related videos and tutorials. His ultimate goal is to establish a prominent presence in the industry and contribute to making the world a more visually appealing place.

As a creative and diligent designer, Jason is committed to delivering exceptional results that meet his clients' needs. He specializes in the art forms of abstract and minimalism, producing designs that are both striking and functional.

In his free time, Jason indulges in his passion for music production, drawing inspiration from his favorite artists like "YOUHA" and "Fly By Midnight". He also enjoys playing video games such as Overwatch, Genshin Impact and Rocket League.
