Bio

Hello world! My name is Jason and I’m a passionate multimedia artist and designer living in the comforts of Melbourne, Australia. I’m currently 16 as a full-time student and in my free time, I work on creating a space to share my designs and content.

When I say I love media and graphic design, I mean I do. I remember participating in many art competitions and I grasped the Adobe apps when I was 13. Since then, creating videos and graphics has been my number one passion. Outside of media and design, I enjoy singing and creating music. Isn’t music considered an art?

In 2019, I launched my YouTube channel and in 2021 I opened my Instagram profile. After countless hours of creating graphic design and other pieces of media, I have gathered a fanbase of over 700 on YouTube and 200 on Instagram. I’ve also worked with many small creators on the platforms helping them boost their view and subscriber count along with designing them crisp and professional profile pictures, thumbnails and banners.

I have slowly found my touch in minimalism and retro styles of design as if I was raised in the 80’s. My photography, graphics and videos all reflect my love towards this movement of design and overall make a lasting image in a client, reminding them how fast time passes now. In my free time, I spend my time listening to synth-wave music and playing some casual video games. I also ponder philosophical questions and write stories and lore about my brand and online persona.

So what are you waiting for? Drop me a message and let’s roll!
