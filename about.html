Bio

Jason is a multimedia designer who resides in Melbourne Australia. Born in 2007 with a Shanghai background, he began learning the piano at 6. At 13, he decided to self-teach himself in graphic design and video editing. In 2019, he created his first YouTube channel with his online name “jhxu” and began to create various types of videos. On the 22nd of April 2023, he created his artist stage name and released the first composition,

In November of 2023, Jason merged ISAZU under jhxu, creating a diverse online identity of creativity. By creating unforgettable and stunning designs, he has made his brand clear in the world by obtaining over a thousand fans on various platforms.

Jason continues studying media, design and music, hoping to one day create a loving community and make the world more visually appealing.
