Bio

Hi there, I'm a multimedia designer residing in Melbourne, Australia. It was at the age of 13, when I stepped into the waters of graphic design and various forms of media. This pavied the way for a self-taught exploration of these artistic domains.

In 2019, I initiated my online presence through my YouTube channel, adopting the online name "jhxu" and experimenting with various video types. In 2023, I launched my music under the name of "ISAZU".

When I was in my final years of high school, I devoted time into content creation and graphic design which led to me to gain over hundreds of fans online like YouTube. Currently, my focus is dedicated to an endless quest for mastering the intricacies of graphic design.
