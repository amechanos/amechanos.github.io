Bio

As creativity meets chaos, a new world begins. With origins in Shanghai, China, Jason was raised in the suburban city of Melbourne. Growing up, he ventured into the creative industry exploring music production and graphic design.

Through 2019, Jason developed a strong passion towards graphic design and started a YouTube channel. After the pandemic hit, his YouTube channel grew, slowly to 800 fans. In 2023, Jason began his official music career, launching it under "ISAZU". With his first single "Autumn's Calling" followed by the one-hit wonder: "Sunshine".

Jason continues studying music and graphic design, hoping to one day create a loving community and make the world more visually appealing.
